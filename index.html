ABOUT

I want to collect all purple, pink, bird, and dragon shiny Pokémon! You may want to know how I define the categories. Of the colors that change between the normal and shiny forms, the majority color or most starking difference must be purple and/or pink. Sprite comparison site. For birds, the Pokémust be a bird design or bird-like in appearance, including but not limited to feathers, wings, beaks, talons, and flight. For the dragon category, the Pokémon must be Dragon-type, evolve into a Dragon-type, or have a Dragon-type Mega Evolution.
